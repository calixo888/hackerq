What is HackerQ?

Attendee Organizer

HackerQ allows hackathon organizers to manage their attendees more efficiently. Attendees can be tracked throughout the registration, attendance, and competition phases of the hackathon, as well as throughout the judging and awards sections of the competition.

News & Update Hub

HackerQ makes it easy for hackathon organizers to send out updates about the competition or reminders that a workshop is starting or that a meal has arrived. This makes it easy for attendees to get notified that something new is happening.

Convenience Tool
